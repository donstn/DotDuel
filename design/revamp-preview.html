How to play

1

Take your turn

On each turn, colour one empty dot — or claim a pending line. Then it's the other player's move.

2

Complete a line, score it

Fill a full run of dots in any valid direction to score points equal to its length.

3

Biggest line wins the move

If a move completes several lines, only the longest scores now. The rest become pending — claim them on a later turn.

Winning

The game ends when every dot is filled and every line is claimed. Highest score wins — equal scores draw.
